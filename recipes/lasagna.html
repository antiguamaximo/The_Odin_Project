BEST Lasagna

Description

This is the recipe for the best lasagna ever. Surprise your friends, or make your fiance think that she has a professional chef at home!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef/Ground Chicken or Sausage
  • Egg
  • Cheese: Mozzarella or Ricotta, or both!
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, black pepper, and Italian seasoning
  • Two Tablespoons of sugar
  • Crushed Tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste
  • Onion and Garlic

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the Noddles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let it cool before serving!
